Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Facebook Bulk Scheduler ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may collect the following information:

  • Facebook Page Information: Page ID, Page name, and Page access tokens that you provide to schedule posts.
  • Content Data: Images, captions, quotes, and hashtags that you upload for scheduling.
  • Schedule Information: Date and time preferences for your scheduled posts.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Scheduling and publishing posts to your Facebook Page on your behalf
  • Displaying previews of your scheduled content
  • Providing the core functionality of the application

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Images are temporarily stored only during the upload process and deleted immediately after posting
  • We do not permanently store your Facebook access tokens on our servers
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your content is only shared with Facebook's API to fulfill the scheduling functionality.

6. Facebook Data

Our App accesses Facebook data through official Facebook APIs. We comply with Facebook's Platform Terms and Developer Policies. The permissions we request include:

  • pages_manage_posts: To publish scheduled posts to your Page
  • pages_read_engagement: To verify connection to your Page
  • pages_show_list: To display your available Pages

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Uploaded images are deleted immediately after being posted to Facebook
  • Session data is cleared when you close the application
  • We do not maintain long-term databases of user content

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke the App's access to your Facebook account at any time through Facebook Settings
  • Request deletion of any data we may have
  • Opt out of using our service at any time

9. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
